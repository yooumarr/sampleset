How to upload Documents:
When the app is running, a section with a button can be viewed. Clicking on the browse button will open your File Explorer and you can select your PDF file. 

Ask Questions:
Once PDF is uploaded correctly, a section named "Enter your query" takes in your questions for the document. You may ask your questions there. 

Interpreting the bot’s responses:
Since the reasoning model used here in this RAG bot isn't very high-fetched, the responses we get are not exactly in the way the should be. Meaning, we have to read the responses and figure out ourselves the answer.
